About Makhan

At Makhan, baking is more than just mixing ingredients – it's about creating moments of joy and bringing people together. Our name, meaning "butter" in Hindi, reflects our commitment to using rich, quality ingredients in everything we make.

Started from our home kitchen with a passion for traditional recipes and modern techniques, we've grown into a beloved local bakery. Every item is handcrafted in small batches to ensure freshness and maintain that authentic homemade taste.

We believe that the best treats are made with love, patience, and the finest ingredients. From our family to yours, we're honored to be part of your celebrations and everyday moments of sweetness.
